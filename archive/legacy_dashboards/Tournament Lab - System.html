Why it matters
At 25–30 bb effective in BTN-vs-SB confrontations, folding too often forfeits a measurable share of the pot equity you've already paid for with your opening range. The shortfall is heaviest against three opponents who three-bet wide on the small blind; against the rest of the field, defense is closer to baseline. Closing this leak is high-leverage because the spot is common and the deviation is large.
