01

Product thesis

the one paragraph

PRODUCT THESIS
A private analysis tool for tournament poker players who treat the grind seriously. We read their hand histories and answer the only question they actually have: am I profitable, and if not — what's the one thing to fix? Money is the language. Range deviations are the proof. Drills close the loop.

Why we exist

Every other analyzer either drowns the player in unitless stats (HUDs) or hides behind GTO theory the player can't afford to study from scratch (solvers). We sit between: diagnosis-first, dollar-coded, ranked by impact, with the option to drill down to ranges and hands when the player wants the proof.

02

Target user

who we design for

Serious, self-coaching MTT players at micro-to-low stakes (~$1–$15 ABI). They've imported thousands of hands, know what VPIP and PFR mean, and want a tool that talks to them like a peer — poker jargon stays, "Villains" stays, "Predators" stays.

PRIMARY · scorza23-type

Self-coaching grinder

250+ tourneys imported, $1–$5 ABI, plays 30/week, evening sessions. Reads strategy content, can't afford a coach. Wants: "is my game leaking money, where, and how do I fix it?"

SECONDARY

Aspirational mid-stakes

Moving up from $5 to $15 ABI. Wants the same answers plus stake-readiness signal: "am I bankrolled and skill-ready for the next stake?"

Who we are NOT for

  • HUD users — they want live overlay during play; we're offline review only.
  • Cash players — we project in tournaments, ICM matters, payouts matter. Different math.
  • Solver power users — we present canned baselines; we don't run sims.
  • High-stakes pros — they already have coaches and bespoke databases.
03

Core promise

what we deliver in 30 seconds

Open the app. Within 30 seconds you know three things:

  1. Are you profitable? — Big green number, lifetime $, plus 30d trend. (Money hero on Dashboard.)
  2. What's the biggest leak? — One named issue, position-tagged, with $/tourney cost and a confidence score. (Top blocker card.)
  3. What to do next? — A single CTA into the matching range view or filtered hand set. (Action buttons.)

What we deliberately don't promise

  • Coaching-level prose. We rank, quantify, and link to the spot. We don't lecture.
  • Solver accuracy. "Baseline · GTO" is a canned reference, clearly labelled as such.
  • Live HUD. Offline review only.
  • Comparison to a global pool. Sample is your own database; we don't fake percentiles we can't see.

Dashboard rules

non-negotiables for the entry page

The Dashboard is the most-seen surface. These rules govern what goes on it and what doesn't.

Composition (in order, top to bottom)

01
Money hero is always the top module. Lifetime $ in --money green, monospace, ≥56px. Sparkline / chip stack lives here, never anywhere else.
firm
02
KPI carousel surfaces 6 of 12 indicators, paginated. VPIP / PFR / 3-bet / AF / WTSD / W$SD first page; positional + by-street second page.
firm
03
Verdict + Range matrix sit side-by-side. Switching the matrix position (BTN/CO/SB/BB) re-writes the verdict copy. They are linked, not independent.
firm
04
Top Blocker + Stack Bands are peers. Two-column row; both ground the verdict in concrete data.
firm
05
Leak Queue is ranked by $/tournament, never bb/100. bb/100 sits as secondary copy. Dollar impact is the headline number.
firm
06
Career arc owns the bottom of the page, not the top. Money hero answers "are you profitable today"; arc answers "how did you get here". They serve different prompts.
firm
07
Diagnostics drawer collapsed by default. 16 metrics. Preview row peeks 8; click expands all 16. Never the entry surface.
firm

What never goes on Dashboard

  • Full hand tables (live on /hands)
  • Full range editor (lives on /ranges)
  • Per-session drill-down (lives on /sessions)
  • Per-villain stats (lives on /villains)
  • Filler stat tiles. Every tile must answer a question a serious player asks weekly.

Interaction rules

  • Scope segment (Lifetime / 30d / Session) re-renders the money hero + KPI carousel + leak queue. Affects everything; never just one module.
  • Range tabs swap the matrix AND the verdict copy. They are bonded.
  • Leak rows expand inline on click — they don't navigate. Navigation is on the "Review hands" / "Open in Ranges" buttons inside the expand.
  • KPI tiles filter on click (selected state). Click again to deselect. Only one selected at a time.

Metrics glossary

canonical definitions · use these names exactly

Money metrics

NameUnitDefinition
Net profit (lifetime)$Sum of buy-ins minus sum of cashes across all imported tournaments. The headline money number. Owner: Dashboard hero.
ROI%Lifetime cashes divided by buy-ins, minus 1. Always shown vs 0% breakeven, never vs a field average.
ITM rate%Tournaments cashed / tournaments played. Reference: ~16% field average (paid spots ratio).
Best cash$Largest single-tournament cash on record, with position + date.
Drawdown$Largest peak-to-trough dip in cumulative profit. Always accompanied by recovery span ("recovered in 12 tournaments").
ABI$Average buy-in across all imported tournaments. Sidebar bankroll card.
$/tournament$/TProjected dollar impact of a leak — bb/100 × ABI × hands per tournament / 100. THE rank-by metric in the Leak Queue.

Play-style indicators (KPIs)

NameUnitDefinition
VPIP%Voluntarily Put $ In Pot. % of hands you put chips in pre-flop excluding blinds posted dead. target band 20–24%
PFR%Pre-Flop Raise. % of hands you raise pre-flop. target band 16–19%
3-bet%% of opportunities you re-raise a pre-flop open. target band 7–9%
4-bet%% of opportunities you re-raise a 3-bet. target band ~3%
AFratioAggression Factor — (bets + raises) / calls post-flop. target band 2–3
WTSD%Went To ShowDown. % of hands seen flop that reached river showdown. target band 22–26%
W$SD%Won $ at ShowDown. % of showdowns won. target band 48–54%

Positional + by-street

NameUnitDefinition
BTN open%% of pots opened from Button. target 48–52%
CO open%% of pots opened from Cutoff. target 34–38%
SB open%% of pots opened from Small Blind. target 40–44%
BB defense%% of opens you defend (call or 3-bet) from Big Blind. target 42–46% vs HJ
Steal%% of pots opened from CO+BTN+SB combined when folded to you. target 39–42%
C-bet flop%% of times you bet the flop after raising pre. target 60–70%
Fold to 3b%% of times you fold to a 3-bet after opening. target 48–56%
ICM ΔbbIndependent Chip Model edge vs baseline play in tournament late-game spots.

Sample / confidence

NameUnitDefinition
n (sample)countNumber of hands matching the filter. ≥500 high · 100–500 medium · <100 low
Confidence0–1Statistical confidence in a leak signal. Function of sample size and effect size. Shown as bar + numeric (e.g. "0.88 · high").
Severitycrit/high/medBucketed dollar impact. CRIT ≥$0.50/T · HIGH $0.15–$0.50/T · MED <$0.15/T
Readiness/100Composite score: sample × compliance × profitability. Used as a single-glance "are you fit to move stake?" signal.
Compliance%% of your decisions that match the active strategy profile (Baseline · GTO by default).

Design decisions

locked-in calls · do not relitigate without cause

Brand & language

D01
"Villains" stays as the opponent term. Poker-native jargon; product is for poker players. Do not rename to "Players" / "Opponents".
locked
D02
"Predators" framing stays. Honest about what the data shows — players who consistently take chips off you. Do not soften to "Top exposures".
locked
D03
Product name = parked. "Tournament Lab" is dead. Working name "Poker Analyzer" until a real one is picked.
deferred

Visual system

D04
Single violet accent (#8b7cff). No neon green chrome. Green (#b8e1c8) is reserved for money.
locked
D05
Severity = dots, not chrome. Status communicated by colored dots / micro-marks. No full-card-colored states.
locked
D06
No glassmorphism on data surfaces. Backdrops are flat panels. Glass only on overlays and sticky chrome.
locked
D07
Mono for numbers, sans for prose. JetBrains Mono on every figure; Inter on every sentence. Mixed labels follow the value's type.
locked

Information architecture

D08
5 modules on Dashboard, nothing deleted. Money hero · KPIs · Verdict+Range · Blocker+Stacks · Leak queue · Career arc · Diagnostics. (Counts the collapsed drawer as one.)
locked
D09
Sidebar is collapsible (⌘\). Expanded default; collapses to icon rail.
locked
D10
Money is the hero. Lifetime $ takes the top slot on Dashboard. Verdict moves below.
locked
D11
Range matrix lives on the Dashboard. Not Ranges-only. 13×13 BTN open is the signature widget; deviant cells highlighted.
locked
D12
Diagnostics is a drawer, not a page. Lives on Dashboard, collapsed by default with 8-tile peek row.
locked

Interaction

D13
Chip stack is data-encoded. Chip count grows/shrinks with profit. Palette tiers at $100/$300/$700/$1500.
locked
D14
KPI tiles are a carousel, not a fixed grid. 6 per page, paged with arrows. Reach beyond 6 indicators without making the row scroll horizontally.
locked
D15
Range tabs drive verdict copy. Switching BTN→CO→SB→BB rewrites both the matrix and the adjacent verdict card.
locked
D16
Leak rows expand in place. No navigation on row click; the row reveals an inline panel with mini distribution + sample hands + actions.
locked

Open questions

unblockers for Leaks page + v1 launch

BLOCKING NEXT BUILD
  1. Leaks page sidebar — keep or hide?Keep gives consistent chrome + bankroll card visible always. Hiding gives ~232px back to the leak detail pane, useful for full-size range overlay. My lean: keep it, but allow collapse via the same ⌘\ shortcut.
  2. Predators framing — side card or inline?Either a sidebar widget on the Leaks page showing villain types that exploit each leak (e.g. "3-bettors · stationy callers"), or inline annotations on each sample hand row. Pick one — having both fragments the signal.
  3. One Leaks page direction, or two as tweakable variations?Default is one solid direction. Two only if you want side-by-side exploration of layout (e.g. left-rail list vs top filter bar). Adds a turn.
v1 LAUNCH BLOCKERS
  1. Product name.Still parked. "Poker Analyzer" works as a working name but not for marketing. Need 1 word, peer-not-vendor energy. Constraint: not "Analyzer", not "HUD", not "EV Platform".
  2. Brand mark.Drop the abstract geometric. Use a poker-native primitive (suit-stack, range-grid 3×3, card-corner, spade-with-rule, chip-ring). Picking name + mark together.
  3. Strategy profile picker — UI scope?D18 says Baseline only in v1, but the chip already says "Baseline · GTO" suggesting alternatives exist. Either commit to single-profile and hide the dropdown affordance, or build the picker now and ship 1 option.
DEFERRABLE
  1. Mobile layout.Design system is desktop-first 1480px. No mobile rules yet. Defer until we know if mobile review is a real use case.
  2. Empty-state for first-time users.What does Dashboard look like with 0 imported hands? Not solved.
  3. Multi-database / multi-stake users.If a user plays $1 AND $10 ABI, do we show two cards or one combined? Not solved.
