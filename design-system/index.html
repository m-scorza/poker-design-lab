Poker Analyzer · Design System · v1

The instrument.

A precision instrument for poker study. Quiet near-black chassis, silver type, structure drawn with hairlines and tone shifts instead of boxes and glow. Color is meaning: green is money won, red is money lost, amber is caution, and violet is you — your cursor, your focus, your position in the app. One jewel per screen may shine. The poker objects themselves are the visual signature.

02 · Identity

The violet signature

Violet marks user presence and wayfinding — where you are, where you act. It never decorates. The cursor halo on this very page is live: 10px, the identity element, with a behavioral roadmap (magnetic, expands over data, snaps to matrix cells).

Violet lives here
  • cursor halo · text selection (try selecting this)
  • focus rings
  • live pulse
  • active nav tick · active tab underline
  • breadcrumb current · CTA arrows · loader
Violet never touches
  • surfaces, panels, borders at rest
  • chart lines, data values, the 13×13 matrix
  • body text, icons, decoration of any kind
  • gradients (the slop signature)
03 · Foundations

Typography — hybrid voice

Bricolage Grotesque for display (the voice), Inter for body (invisible), JetBrains Mono for every numeral, kicker and data cell. Treatment carries the identity: huge display-to-label contrast, tight display tracking, uppercase mono kickers, tabular numerals everywhere.

04 · Structure

Surfaces — tone shifts, hairlines, one jewel

Compartments are tone shifts with no borders, no glass, no glow. Internal structure is 1px hairlines. Exactly one jewel per screen may keep glass + glow — on the Desk it's lifetime profit, glowing green because it's money.

06 · Components

Controls & semantics

Primary action is white-on-ink (no colored buttons). The arrow glyph carries the signature. Active states are marked by violet ticks, not color floods. Semantic chips use soft + line pairs.
