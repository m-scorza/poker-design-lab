Each block enters with a staggered fade-up as it crosses the viewport — IntersectionObserver with a 55 ms per-batch stagger and an anti-hidden-content failsafe.

001 · Premise

Content that breathes as it enters

The observer fires once per element. When the bottom edge crosses −8% of the viewport, the block animates and stops being observed — no continuous scroll cost.

002 · Stagger

Staggered batches

Neighboring elements inherit a growing transition-delay (up to 6 steps × 55 ms), so a group entering together cascades instead of flashing in all at once.

003 · Resilience

Never leaves anything hidden

It respects prefers-reduced-motion and, if the observer never fires, a 1.4 s failsafe forces all blocks to appear. The content is always legible.
